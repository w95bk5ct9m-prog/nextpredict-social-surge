TimingTue-Thu, 8-10am or 12-2pm local. Stagger posts by at least 1-2 days - it keeps the feed alive longer.
First lineLinkedIn shows 2-3 lines before "see more". The opener is the scroll-stopper - don't change it without a reason.
Engage fastReply to every comment within 60 mins. LinkedIn's algorithm rewards early engagement velocity.
Make it yoursIf a phrase doesn't sound like you, rewrite it. Authenticity beats polish on LinkedIn every time.
Attach the imageUpload the JPG when composing. Posts with images get significantly more reach than text-only.
